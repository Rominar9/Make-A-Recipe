Arroz Con Leche

Arroz con leche translate in english is called rice pudding, it's a quick, easy and tasty snack that anyone can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Condence milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • 2 cinnamon stick

Method:

  1. First, let one cup of rice simmer in a bowl of water for 5 minute.
  2. While the rice is simmering add in 2 cups of water to boil in a pot.
  3. Add in two pieces of cinnamon stick into the boiling water and stir.
  4. After the water comes to a boil add in the rice and let it cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the evaporated milk, condence milk, vanilla extract and stir.
  6. Let it boil, and taste the rice to see if it's soft or to your liking.
  7. Make sure to turn of the stove and let the rice pudding cool down.
  8. Lastly, top it off with some cinnamon on top and it's ready to serve.
